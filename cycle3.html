What I did



This cycle, we are learning about garment printing, uv printing, and sublimation. Our first challenge

this cycle is to make a shirt that has something to do with Hudson. I am making a shirt that says "HUDSON"

and an outline of Ohio will be in the middle, with the clock tower on Hudson. The shirt came out very well.

The second class I made and printed some flip flops with American flag by sublimation. Sublimation is when

you heat the ink on paper hot enough to make it a gas, it transfers to the print surface, and cools into a solid again

My design turned out very well and transferred better than I could've asked.

Finally, I made a golf ball that says "DAMN." on it because after 90% of my hits I say "damn" because I am bad.

This was put no the golf ball through UV printing. It took about 15 minutes total.
