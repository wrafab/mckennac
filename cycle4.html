Cycle 4

What I did



This cycle was full of mess ups for me. We had been tasked to make a clock using the laser cutter,

make a press fit kit on the engraver, then engrave a glass or water bottle. My clock took 4 attempts

to print correctly. The first time, I cut out too much. The second time I cut out too little. The third

time the power went out half way through. Finally, it worked on the fourth try. It came out as planned

and looks how I want. I also had difficulty making my press fit kit. I did not cut out at all how I wanted.

I also messed up on my cup engraving and the image got wrapped around the sides and it looked terrible.
