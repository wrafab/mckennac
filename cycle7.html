Cycle 7

What I did



This week was a fun week in Digital Fab. We had to make a longboard and CNC Mill a mold for chocolate.

I have not filled my mold with chocolate, but my longboard is finished and turned out very well.

I decided I wanted an outer space theme, so I spray painted the whole thing myself, then ordered some purple wheels with black trucks to complete the look.

I didn't run into any difficulties or serious problems, my only setbacks were waiting for the paint booth to be open and having to redrill the holes for my trucks a few times to get the right size.

Colin McKenna
