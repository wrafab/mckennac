GRILL CLUB

Grill club is a club where if it can be eaten, we will grill it. Whether it be meats, fruits, or vegetables, it will be grilled.
We plan to meet as a large group every 6 weeks or so. Along with that, we will take smaller groups of 15 or so and meet every 3 weeks to try new recipes and have a more personalized experience.
